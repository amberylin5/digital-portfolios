Wearify - Chinese Culture Fashion App

Technologies: Adobe XD

Category: Mobile App, UI/UX Design

Date: June 2023

Project Overview

Wearify is a mobile application designed to outspread Chinese culture through fashion. The app allows users to explore traditional and modern Chinese-inspired outfits, book studio sessions for professional photoshoots, and connect with designers specializing in Chinese cultural attire.

Key Features

  • Interactive catalog of Chinese cultural fashion items
  • Studio booking system with calendar integration
  • Community section for sharing outfit ideas

Development Process

The project began with extensive user research to understand the needs of people interested in Chinese cultural fashion. I created wireframes and prototypes in Adobe XD and conducted several rounds of user testing.

Challenges & Solutions

One major challenge was implementing the features with good performance on mid-range devices. I optimized the 3D models and used a progressive loading system to ensure smooth operation across different device capabilities.
